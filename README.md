#  Dr. Soma Sekhar Pulamarasetti, Pharm.D.
###  Assistant Professor | BR Nahata College of Pharmacy, Mandsaur University

---

##  About Me
I am a clinical researcher and academic specializing in pharmacy practice. My work focuses on bridging the gap between clinical pharmacy practice and structured clinical trial operations through data-driven insights. I am particularly interested in clinical trial methodology, clinical data management, and the operational workflows of multinational clinical trial frameworks.

---

##  Research Interests
* **Clinical Trial Methodology:** Optimizing clinical protocol design, operational workflows, and trial coordination frameworks for multicenter and multinational clinical trials.
* **Clinical Data Management:** Utilizing data-driven insights to monitor safety endpoints, efficacy variables, and real-world clinical outcomes.
* **Advanced Life Sciences:** Investigating systemic biomedical and physiological frameworks to improve clinical outcomes and research translation (CSIR NET Qualified).

---

## Technical Toolkit & Skills
* **Programming & Version Control:** R (Clinical Data Manipulation, Data Visualization, & Scripting), Git, and GitHub for reproducible research workflows.
* **Data Management & Analysis:** Statistical Analysis (SPSS), Clinical Data Collection & Database Verification (Microsoft Excel, Google Forms).
* **Research Methodologies:** Protocol Development, Case Record Form (CRF) Design, Good Clinical Practice (GCP) Compliance, and Cross-Sectional Observational Study Designs.
